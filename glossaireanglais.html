Glossary

Feminism is a movement that is becoming movement that is more and more known but is still for many people.

Discover a glossary with some important important terms of feminism.

Feminism

It is a movement that fights against inequalities between women and men. We seek to put in the woman in order to improve her rights and her vision in our society.

Ecofeminist

It is simply the mixture between the ecological movement and the feminist movement. We consider that the two fights are linked. Vandana Shiva is a figure of ecofeminism (See Gallery).

Equality

Word designating something equal. In relation to humans, we would say that they are equal if no one was discriminated.

Privilege

A privilege is an advantage given to someone or something. Often this word is attributed to things that are done against the general laws. In feminism, men are considered to have privileges over women.

Law

It is the set of measures that a state defines for its citizens that defines their statue.

Patriarchy

It is a societal representation where the man has all the power in a family. The woman and the child have less authority than the man.

Misogyny

It is the act of having hatred or contempt for women. Most of the time, this term is given by the hatred of men against women.

LGBT

This acronym represents people who are not heterosexual. The acronym has many variants (LGBTQ+ / LGBTQIA+ / etc).
